# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project
a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior includes:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting
the maintainer at **safeitexperts@safeitexperts.com**.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
